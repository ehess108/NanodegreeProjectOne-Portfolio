About Me

My best qualities are thoroughness and accuracy, and supervisors appreciate my conscientiousness and willingness to help others. I am both creative and analytical, and I love opportunities to challenge myself and grow.

I created my first website using Wordpress to standout when applying for jobs. I really enjoyed it, but I started to crave the creative freedom that writing my own code provides. After earning my CompTIA A+ Certification, I applied for a Grow with Google Scholarship to study Front-End Web Development at Udacity. I was pleasantly surprised to be invited to phase one, which was a challenge course. When I started, I thought to want to be a developer was like wanting to be a rock star. However, when I was selected to go onto phase two, a full scholarship to the Nanodegree Program, I started to believe that my passion could turn into a career.

I try to live life mindfully and compassionately. I believe connecting with nature allows me to find my inner guidance. My kid is a Mini Schnauzer named Zoe, and we enjoy walking in the woods. I am currently writing a book about finding my North Star.
